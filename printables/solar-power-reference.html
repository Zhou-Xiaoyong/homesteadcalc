← Back to All Printables

A one-page cheat sheet for sizing off-grid solar: appliance wattages, what an array actually produces, how big a battery bank you need, and realistic example systems.

⚡ MPPT > PWM

An MPPT charge controller harvests up to 30% more energy than PWM, especially in cold weather and with higher-voltage arrays.

🧮 The 20% Rule

After calculating your loads, add 20–30% headroom for cloudy stretches, battery aging, and the loads you haven't thought of yet.

🔥 Heat ≠ Solar

Resistance heating (space heaters, electric water heaters) devours batteries. Use propane, wood, or diesel for heat off-grid.

📐 Tilt & Direction

Face true south (northern hemisphere); tilt ≈ your latitude. Winter: latitude +15°. Even flat-mounted panels lose only ~10–15% annually.
