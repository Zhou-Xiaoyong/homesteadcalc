How It Works

This calculator uses feeding standards from the American Sheep Industry Association and American Goat Federation. Requirements vary based on animal class, weight, and production stage.

Feed Requirements by Class

  • Dry Animals: 2.5–3% of body weight in forage daily
  • Lactating: 4–5% of body weight plus grain supplementation
  • Kids/Lambs: 5–7% of body weight, increasing grain as they grow
  • Meat Production: 3–4% of body weight with grain finishing
Daily Hay = Weight × % of Body Weight × Number of Animals Daily Grain = (Weight × Grain Factor) × Number of Animals Annual Hay (tons) = Monthly Hay × 12 ÷ 2000 Monthly Cost = (Monthly Hay ÷ 2000 × Hay Price) + (Monthly Grain ÷ 50 × Grain Price)
