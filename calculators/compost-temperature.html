How It Works

This tracker is based on research from USDA Extension Service and compost science. Temperature is the primary indicator of decomposition activity and pathogen destruction.

Compost Temperature Stages

  • Mesophilic (below 104°F): Initial stage, slow decomposition
  • Thermophilic (104–160°F): Active stage, pathogen kill zone
  • Cooling (below 104°F after heating): Maturation stage
Pathogen Kill = Temperature ≥ 131°F for 3 consecutive days Optimal Range = 130–150°F (balanced decomposition) Turn Frequency = Every 3–7 days during thermophilic stage
