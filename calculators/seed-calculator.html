How It Works

This calculator helps you determine exactly how many seeds to buy based on your garden size and crop type. It factors in germination rates and recommended plant spacing.

Key Factors

  • Germination Rate: Not all seeds will sprout — older seeds have lower rates
  • Spacing: Each crop has optimal spacing requirements
  • Transplant vs Direct: Starting indoors reduces seed waste
Seeds Needed = (Plants Required ÷ Germination Rate) × (1 + Extra %) Plants Required = Planting Area × Plants per Sq Ft
