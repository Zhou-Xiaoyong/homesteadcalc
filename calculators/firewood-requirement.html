How It Works

This calculator is based on USDA Forest Service guidelines and heating degree day calculations. Firewood requirements depend on home size, insulation, climate, and heating usage.

Key Terms

  • Standard Cord: 128 cubic feet (4x4x8 ft stack)
  • Face Cord: 1/3 of a standard cord (4x8 ft face)
  • Heating Degree Days: Measure of coldness based on 65°F baseline
Cords Needed = (Home Size × Climate Factor × Insulation Factor × Heating Factor) ÷ Efficiency Standard Cord = 4ft × 4ft × 8ft = 128 cubic feet 1 Tree ≈ 1/2 to 1 cord (depending on size)
