Common Reasons to Contact Us

Suggest a Calculator
Have an idea for a tool that would make homestead planning easier? We're always looking for new calculators to build. Tell us what you need and we'll add it to our roadmap.
Report a Bug
Found something that doesn't look right — a wrong number, a broken layout, or a confusing result? Let us know the calculator name, what you entered, and what you expected to see.
Partnership Inquiry
Interested in collaborating on content, data, or tools? We're open to working with extension offices, homestead educators, and like-minded organizations.
General Feedback
Just want to share how a calculator helped you plan your homestead? We love hearing success stories. They keep us motivated to build more.
